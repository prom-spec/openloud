OpenLoud turns any ebook into an audiobook using text-to-speech. Import your books, pick a voice, and listen.

Features:
• Import EPUB, PDF, TXT, DOCX, FB2, ODT, and MOBI files
• Device TTS (offline) or Edge TTS AI neural voices
• Volume boost for quiet audio
• Android Auto support — browse and play from your car
• Remembers your position in each book
• Chapter navigation with skip forward/back
• Dark theme

OpenLoud is free and open source (GPL-3.0).
